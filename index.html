Travel

LaGuardia Airport (LGA)

From LGA, take the free LaGuardia Link Q70 shuttle to Roosevelt Ave/74th Street stop (about 10 minutes, non-stop). Then, walk one block to the 74 St-Broadway Subway stop and take the 7 Train to Grand Central - 42 Street (about 20 minutes). You can also take the F Train from Jackson Heights - Roosevelt Ave to 42 Street - Bryant Park (about 17 minutes) and then walk to Grand Central Terminal (about 8 minutes).

From Grand Central Terminal, take the Hudson Metro North Train to Hastings-on-Hudson (about 55 minutes), conveniently located two miles from the hotel and a six minute walk from Harvest.

Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR)

From EWR, take the Newark Airport Express Bus to Grand Central Terminal in Manhattan (about 70 minutes).

From Grand Central Terminal, take the Hudson Metro North train to Hastings-on-Hudson (about 55 minutes), conveniently located two miles from the hotel and a six minute walk from Harvest.

Westchester County Airport (HPN)

HPN is the closest airport of the three options, just a 25 minute Uber or taxi ride from the hotel.
